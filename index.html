Site Speed is the New SEO

The 2020 pandemic catalysed a rapid shift from in-person shopping to online shopping. Majority of in-person services also moved online and adopted Zoom meetings and electronic communication, resulting in consumers spending more time researching online about the product or the service they plan to purchase. As never before, the essential web vitals, such as page speed load, became a crucial factor in business success. Latest Google Analytics and Google Ads statistics demonstrate that slow websites are more likely to produce higher bounce rates and lower conversions, meaning that speed load milliseconds could mean millions in missed sales.
